Our Story of Excellence Since 2018

Fortivo: Premium Plywood Manufacturer & India's Leading Plyboard Supplier

Welcome to Fortivo, a premier plywood supplier in India known for its extensive range of high-quality plywood designed for a wide variety of industries and applications. Our journey began in 2018 in the vibrant city of Kochi, Kerala, with a clear mission- to deliver plywood products that stand out for their durability, reliability, and sustainability.

At Fortivo, we believe that great interiors begin with strong foundations. Guided by our motto, “Built to Last,” we are committed to producing plywood that delivers exceptional strength, long-term performance, and consistent quality. As a top plywood manufacturer, we leverage advanced manufacturing processes to ensure every sheet of wood meets rigorous industrial and aesthetic standards.

Our facility in Kottayam, Kerala uses only the finest hardwood and Gurjan species. Whether you are searching for waterproof plywood for durable furniture or commercial plywood for large-scale construction, Fortivo provides the reliable foundation your projects need.

Our Vision

To become a leading global brand in high-quality plywood and interior solutions by providing eco-friendly and sustainable wood products.

Our Commitment

Ensuring every sheet is crafted with precision, attention to detail, and a focus on long-lasting performance for customers worldwide.
