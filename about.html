About Fortivo

Fortivo is a trusted plywood supplier based in Veliyannoor, Kottayam, Kerala, delivering high-quality plywood for construction and interior applications. Guided by our motto “Built to Last,” we focus on strength, durability, and consistent quality.

Our range includes Semi Hardwood, Alternate Gurjan, Okoume Alternate, Gurjan Semi, Full Red Core, and Rubberwood plywood, all ISI 303 certified to meet Indian quality standards. Designed for furniture, kitchens, cabinetry, and interiors, Fortivo plywood offers reliability, stability, and long-lasting performance.

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark for premium plywood, setting new standards in durability and aesthetic excellence for modern architecture.

Our Commitment

We are committed to sustainable sourcing and precision engineering, ensuring every sheet of Fortivo plywood is a masterpiece of reliability.

Our Materials

From the core veneers to the face finish, only the finest hardwood and Gurjan species are chosen to bear the Fortivo seal of quality.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our manufacturing facility uses state-of-the-art technology to ensure precision in thickness, bonding strength, and moisture control. Every batch undergoes rigorous testing to meet our "Built to Last" promise.

We specialize in different types of cores, from Semi Hardwood to Alternate Gurjan, catering to both industrial construction and luxury interior designs.
